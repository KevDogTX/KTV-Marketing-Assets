Your 6-Phase Implementation Plan

1

Foundation & Identity

Establish consistent business information across all platforms so AI can identify you accurately.

  • NAP consistency audit (Name, Address, Phone)
  • Google Business Profile optimization
  • Directory listings cleanup
  • Business name standardization
  • Local citations verification
2

Technical Infrastructure

Ensure your website meets speed and technical requirements for AI crawling and user experience.

  • Site speed optimization (<3 seconds)
  • Mobile responsiveness
  • Core Web Vitals improvement
  • SSL certificate installation
  • CDN implementation
3

Schema & Structure

Implement structured data that helps AI understand your business, services, and expertise.

  • LocalBusiness schema markup
  • FAQ schema (critical for AI)
  • Review/Rating schema
  • Service/Product schema
  • Article & HowTo schema
4

AI-Friendly Content

Create comprehensive Q&A content that directly answers what prospects ask AI tools.

  • 30+ FAQ questions & answers
  • Service-specific guides
  • Location-based content
  • Problem-solution articles
  • Cost & process explanations
5

Authority Building

Establish trust signals that AI systems use to evaluate credibility and expertise.

  • Review generation system
  • Credentials & certifications display
  • Case studies & testimonials
  • Industry association listings
  • Media mentions & PR
6

AI-Specific Features

Implement advanced features that give you an edge in AI recommendations.

  • LLMs.txt file creation
  • Voice search optimization
  • Natural language content
  • Semantic keyword integration
  • AI citation tracking
